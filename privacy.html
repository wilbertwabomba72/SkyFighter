Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

Overview

Jumping Red & Blue is a single-player rhythm arcade game. The game can be played offline and does not require an account. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be processed when you use the app.

Information We Process

The app stores gameplay progress locally on your device, such as unlocked levels, stars, music settings, and level progress. This information is used only to provide the game experience.

The app may use the device Identifier for Vendor (IDFV) to initialize security and anti-abuse functionality. The app may also request App Tracking Transparency permission on supported iOS versions before initializing advertising services.

Advertising

The app may show rewarded advertisements, such as optional revive ads. Advertising SDKs may process device information, advertising identifiers if permitted, approximate location derived from network data, ad interaction data, diagnostics, and similar technical information to provide, measure, and improve ads.

The app currently integrates advertising services that may include Chartboost, Yandex Mobile Ads, Liftoff/Vungle, and InMobi. Ad availability depends on configuration and network availability.

Security

The app integrates a security SDK to help protect the app from abuse, tampering, fraud, and automated misuse. The security SDK may process technical device signals, app identifiers, and the IDFV for security purposes.

Children's Privacy

The app is designed as a casual game and does not require users to create accounts or submit personal information. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can review the issue.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information may be processed by third-party SDK providers only for advertising, measurement, fraud prevention, security, diagnostics, and service operation purposes.

Your Choices

You can disable personalized tracking by denying the App Tracking Transparency prompt or changing permissions in iOS Settings. You can also reset or limit advertising identifiers using iOS privacy settings where available.

Contact

For privacy or support questions, email jumpingredblueballs.support@gmail.com.
