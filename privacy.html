Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Sky Fighter handles information when you use the game.

Game Data

Sky Fighter stores game progress, unlocked levels, star ratings, high scores, and music preferences locally on your device. The game does not require an account to play.

Tracking Permission

On supported Apple devices, Sky Fighter may request App Tracking Transparency permission. Your device controls whether this permission is granted or denied. If permission is denied or restricted, the game remains playable.

Advertising

Sky Fighter uses advertising technology to request ad placement configuration and load ads. Ads may be shown before later missions, and rewarded ads may be offered for an in-game revive. Advertising partners may process device, ad interaction, diagnostic, and measurement information according to their own policies and the permissions available on your device.

The current advertising integrations include Chartboost, Yandex Mobile Ads, Vungle, and InMobi.

Security SDK

The game includes a security SDK used to support request integrity and anti-abuse protection for supported service calls.

Network Use

The core game content and local progress do not require an account. Network access may be used for advertising configuration, ad delivery, SDK initialization, security-related service calls, and related diagnostics.

Children

Sky Fighter is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children directly through an account or profile system.

Your Choices

  • You can manage tracking permission in the privacy settings of your Apple device.
  • You can choose whether to watch a rewarded revive ad when that option is available.
  • You can remove locally stored progress by deleting the app from your device.

Changes

This policy may be updated when the game, SDK integrations, or legal requirements change. The effective date above identifies the current version.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the support channel provided on the Sky Fighter App Store product page.
